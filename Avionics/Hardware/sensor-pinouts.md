Button Load Cell (1000kg Loadstar Sensors)

| Connector Pin | Wire Color | Assignmnet   |
|---------------|------------|--------------|
| 1             | RED        | Excitation + |
| 2             | GREEN      | Signal +     |
| 3             | BLACK      | Excitation - |
| 4             | WHITE      | Signal -     |
| 5             | SHIELD     | Shield       |
